# COH Cheat Sheet

**`Opening Ceremony`**  
`(have the scouts offstage, ready holding the flags. The US Flag is on the right, then Troop flag on the far left.)` 

`Leader: "Troop, Attention!" Leader: "Audience, Please Rise!" Leader: "Scout Salute! Those not in uniform, please place your right hand over your heart. Color Guard, Advance!" (wait for color guard to reach the front) Leader: "Color Guard, Post the Flag of Troop 99!" Leader: "Please face the United States flag and recite the Pledge of Allegiance. I pledge allegiance…" Leader: "Two!" (Everyone drops salute) Leader: "Scout Sign!" (Everyone raises right hand making the Scout sign) Leader: "Please join me in reciting the Scout Oath and Law. On my honor…” Leader: "Two!" (Everyone drops Scout sign) Leader: "Color Guard, Post the colors!" Leader: "Audience, please be seated!"`
