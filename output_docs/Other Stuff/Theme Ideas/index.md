**Troop Monthly Program Themes **



### **January ? cooking **

**Cooking dawg**



### **February ? Citizenship & Leadership **

**Focus:** Flag etiquette, government structure, community service, troop leadership.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Citizenship in the Nation, Community, and World; Communications.



### **March ? Knots & Lashings **

**Focus:** Basic and advanced knots, lashings, pioneering projects.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Pioneering, Camping.



### **April ? Camping & Outdoor Ethics **

**Focus:** Packing gear, campsite selection, Leave No Trace, meal planning.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Camping, Cooking, Outdoor Ethics.



### **May ? Map & Compass / Orienteering **

**Focus:** Map reading, compass skills, GPS usage, orienteering course.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Orienteering, Hiking.



### **June ? Aquatics & Water Safety **

**Focus:** Swimming skills, water rescues, boating safety, BSA swim test.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Swimming, Lifesaving, Canoeing, Kayaking.



### **July ? Nature & Conservation **

**Focus:** Plant and animal ID, conservation projects, ecology hikes.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Environmental Science, Nature, Forestry, Sustainability.



### **August ? Survival Skills **

**Focus:** Shelter building, fire starting without matches, signaling, water purification.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Wilderness Survival, Search & Rescue.



### **September ? Fire Safety & Tools **

**Focus:** Safe fire building, Totin' Chip skills \(knife, axe, saw\), campfire cooking.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Fire Safety, Woodwork, Camping.



### **October ? STEM & Innovation **

**Focus:** Basic coding, hands-on experiments, Scout STEM Nova award projects.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Programming, Robotics, Engineering, Inventing.



### **November ? Physical Fitness & Personal Growth **

**Focus:** Fitness assessments, goal setting, healthy lifestyle choices.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Personal Fitness, Athletics, Sports.



### **December ? Winter Skills / Cold Weather Camping **

**Focus:** Cold weather gear, hypothermia prevention, snow shelters, indoor skill review.

**Merit Badge Tie-ins:** Snow Sports, Camping.
